This is the top-\/level repository for the \mbox{\hyperlink{classSynaesthetiQ}{SynaesthetiQ}} project.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classSynaesthetiQ}{SynaesthetiQ}} project aims to build a real-\/time, intelligent music visualiser.



\mbox{\hyperlink{classSynaesthetiQ}{SynaesthetiQ}} adds another dimension to your experience of music. \mbox{\hyperlink{classSynaesthetiQ}{SynaesthetiQ}} connects to your home sound system and analyses songs in real-\/time to create mesmerising and tantalising visualisations. The colour and brightness of lights will be influenced by the full spectrum of sound.

You can use \mbox{\hyperlink{classSynaesthetiQ}{SynaesthetiQ}} for\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Brightening up a house party
\item Relaxed Home listening
\item Visualise music for those with auditory impairments.
